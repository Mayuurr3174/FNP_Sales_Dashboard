# 🌸 Ferns and Petals (FNP) Sales Analysis Dashboard

> **Analyzing gift sales data across occasions to uncover revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance.**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from **FNP (Ferns and Petals)** — a gifting company specializing in occasions like Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries. Using **Microsoft Excel**, raw transactional data across orders, customers, and products was transformed into a fully interactive dashboard answering 10 key business questions.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

| # | Question |
|---|---|
| 1 | What is the **total revenue** generated? |
| 2 | What is the **average order & delivery time**? |
| 3 | How do **monthly sales** fluctuate across 2023? |
| 4 | Which are the **top products by revenue**? |
| 5 | What is the **average customer spending**? |
| 6 | What is the **sales performance of top 5 products**? |
| 7 | Which are the **top 10 cities by number of orders**? |
| 8 | Does **order quantity impact delivery time**? |
| 9 | How does **revenue compare across occasions**? |
| 10 | Which **products are most popular** per occasion? |

## 📈 Key Insights

- 🎂 **Raksha Bandhan & Anniversary** drive the highest revenue among all occasions
- 📅 **February and August** are peak revenue months — aligned with Valentine's Day and Raksha Bandhan
- 🏆 **Magnam Set & Quia Gift** are the top 5 bestselling products by revenue
- 🕐 **Evening hours (16–22)** see the highest order placement activity
- 🏙️ **Bhagalpur, Imphal & Kavali** lead in order volume among top 20 cities
- 📦 Average delivery time of **5.53 days** with no significant impact from order quantity

## 🛠️ Tools Used

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Microsoft Excel** | Data cleaning, analysis, dashboard design |
| **Pivot Tables** | Aggregation by occasion, category, month, city |
| **Power Query** | Data transformation and relationship building |

| **Slicers** | Interactive filters — Occasion, Order Date, Delivery Date |

---

## 🗃️ Dataset Details

- **orders.csv** — Order ID, Customer ID, Product ID, Quantity, Order Date, Delivery Date, Location, Occasion
- **customers.csv** — Customer ID, Name, City, Gender, Contact
- **products.csv** — Product ID, Name, Category, Price (INR), Occasion

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open `Complete_Dashboard_Excel.xlsx` in **Microsoft Excel 2016 or later**
3. Use the **slicers** on the right panel to filter by Occasion, Order Date, or Delivery Date
4. All charts update dynamically based on your selection
